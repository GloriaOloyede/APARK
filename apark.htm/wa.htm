WHACK-A-MOLE
A classic game where players use a mallet to hit mechanical moles that pop up randomly. It's fast-paced, requiring quick reflexes to score points by hitting as many moles as possible before time runs out. The game often draws crowds because of its fun, chaotic energy and bright, appealing design.
Play responsibly and be mindful of safety rules to ensure a fun and accident-free experience for everyone.
