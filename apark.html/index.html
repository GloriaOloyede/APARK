RING TOSS
Players toss rings aiming to land them around bottles or pegs arranged at varying distances. The game tests accuracy and skill, with prizes awarded for successful tosses. Its simplicity and the allure of winning make it a popular choice among visitors of all ages.
Play responsibly and be mindful of safety rules to ensure a fun and accident-free experience for everyone.
